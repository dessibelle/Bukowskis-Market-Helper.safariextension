Bukowskis Market Helper

Bukowskis Market Helper is a Safari Extension that helps you with the bidding by automatically calculating the total price (i.e. including commission and hammer fee), when hovering certain prices or placing bids on bukowskismarket.com. That way, when you are hovering a current bid it will be temporarily replaced by the total amount you'd have to pay for the item in question. When you are placing your bid, a total price will also appear next to the bid amount input field.
